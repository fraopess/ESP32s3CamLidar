Protocol Analysis for MTF-02
============================

Expected (from micolink.h/c for MTF-01):
- Header: 0xEF
- Format: [HEAD][DEV_ID][SYS_ID][MSG_ID][SEQ][LEN][PAYLOAD...][CHECKSUM]

Observed bytes:
- 0x00, 0x58, 0x1F, 0x04, 0x6D, 0xFF, 0x14, 0x4F, 0x20, 0x2D, 0xEA, 0x1C, 0x47

Possibilities:
1. Different baud rate (try: 9600, 19200, 38400, 57600, 115200, 230400, 460800, 921600)
2. Different header byte (not 0xEF)
3. Completely different protocol

Recommendation:
- Get MTF-02 datasheet/protocol documentation
- Try common baud rates
- Capture hex dump to analyze pattern
